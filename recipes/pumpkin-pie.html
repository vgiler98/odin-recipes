Pumpkin Pie

Prepare for the lovely holiday of Thanksgiving with this classic tradition pie. Once you follow this recipe, you will be the talk of the town.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 can Libby's 100% pure pumpkin
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1 unbaked 9-inch deep-dish pie shell

Steps

  1. Mix sugar, cinnamon, salt, ginger, and cloves in small bowl. Beat eggs in large bowl. stir in pumpkin and sugar-spice mixture. Gadually stir in evaporated milk
  2. Pour into pie shell
  3. Bake in preheated 425 degrees F oven for 15 minutes. Reduce temperature to 350 degrees F; bake for 40 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean. Cool on wire rack for two houts. Serve immediately or refrigerate.
